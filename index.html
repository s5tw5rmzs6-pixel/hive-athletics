Our Mission

More than a gym.

Hive Athletics gives athletes a place to improve with specialized coaching, filmed sessions, progression tracking, open gyms, scrimmages, tournaments, and community events. The goal is simple: help players grow on and off the court.

Programs

Built for player development.

Training Sessions

Scoring, playmaking, defense, conditioning, and full-court scrimmage training.

Progress Tracking

Coaches document player growth and upload filmed sessions for review.

Open Gym & Scrimmages

Organized runs, competitive games, and affordable access to quality courts.

The Culture
